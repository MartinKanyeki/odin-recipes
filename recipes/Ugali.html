Ugali Recipe

What is Ugali?

Ugali is a common and most popular kenyan dish made from maize flour.

Ugali is best served hot with accompaniments such as fried green vegetables, roasted or fried meat.

Ingredients

  • Maize flour
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil water in a metallic pot
  2. Add the flour while stirring using a cooking stick
  3. Mix still it forms a thick paste
  4. Keep turning and mixing the thick paste so that it evenly cooks
  5. After 20-15 minutes the ugali will be ready
  6. Put a flat cover on the pot
  7. Remove the pot from the fire and carefully overturn it so that the Ugali will lie on the pot cover
  8. Place it on a flat surface while overturned, your Ugali is now ready to be served
