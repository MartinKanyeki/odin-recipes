Nyama Choma Recipe

What is Nyama Choma?

Nyama choma is the swahili name for charcoal roasted meat

This is a Kenyan delicacy which is mostly prepared in social places and at at homes during social gatherings

Nyama choma is commonly prepared by roasting pork, mutton or beef

Ingredients

  • Pork, beef or mutton
  • Salt
  • Berbecue sauce
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Place your meat on the wire mesh that is placed on the charcoal jiko.
  2. Let the meat turn golden brown then keep turning till all sides are golden brown.
  3. Sprinkle some salty water on top to give the meet some salty flavour.
  4. You can also apply some spice eg pepper on the meat, berbecue sauce also can be used as an alternative.
  5. Caut deep into the meet to see that the inner parts are well cooked.
  6. Wrap your meet with an alluminium foil to keep it hot, your meat is ready to be served.
